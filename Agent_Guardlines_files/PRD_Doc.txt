Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Product Name (Working)

Demo Copilot
AI-powered system that generates cinematic, launch-ready product demo videos automatically from real software.

1. Vision

To enable any builder to instantly transform a working product into a high-quality, emotionally compelling demo video that clearly communicates value, without needing video editing skills, storytelling expertise, or manual recording.

In the long term, Demo Copilot becomes the default “product storytelling layer” for software creation and distribution.

2. Problem Statement

Most startups and builders struggle not with building products, but with communicating them effectively.

Key failures:

Product demos are slow to produce and often delayed or skipped
Founders lack storytelling and video editing skills
Existing tools require manual recording and editing effort
Product value is often unclear in early-stage presentations
Launch videos are inconsistent in quality and impact
Technical products are especially hard to explain visually

Result:
Even strong products fail to convert attention into understanding.

3. Solution Overview

Demo Copilot automatically generates polished product demo videos by:

Understanding the product (via URL, repo, or local app)
Identifying key user flows and value points
Generating a structured narrative script
Automatically recording product interactions
Creating voice narration using AI
Rendering a cinematic demo video

Output:
A ready-to-use product demo suitable for:

Product Hunt launches
Investor pitches
Landing pages
Social media campaigns
Onboarding flows
4. Goals

14. Risks & Challenges
1. Poor Product Understanding

If AI misinterprets flows, demos become misleading.

Mitigation:

iterative AI planning loop
fallback manual scene editing
2. Low Video Quality

If rendering is weak, product loses credibility.

Mitigation:

strict cinematic templates
controlled design system
3. Automation Fragility

Web apps vary widely in structure.

Mitigation:

robust Playwright fallback strategies
hybrid manual + AI guidance mode
4. Latency in Generation

Full pipeline may take time.

Mitigation:

staged generation
background processing
